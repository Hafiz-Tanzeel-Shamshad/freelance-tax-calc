🔔 Quick Answers

How long do replies take?

We respond within 2–3 business days. During peak tax season (March–April), it may take up to 5 business days.

How do I contact you directly?

Email us anytime at hafiztanzeel.pk@gmail.com. We respond within 2–3 business days.

Can I request a new calculator?

Absolutely! Use the form to suggest a new tool. We prioritize requests that help the most freelancers and gig workers.

Do you offer tax preparation services?

No. FreelanceTaxCalc provides free calculators and educational content only. We do not prepare taxes or offer personalized financial advice.

How do I report inaccurate tax data?

Please let us know if you spot outdated or incorrect tax information. Include the specific tool and the data you believe is wrong so we can investigate.
